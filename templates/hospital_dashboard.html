Welcome to the Payton Hub

Follow these 4 simple steps to process your patients seamlessly.

1. Create a New Intake

Go to the "1. Start Eligibility" tab. Fill in the patient's medical details, approx cost, and upload their mandatory KYC and Policy documents.

2. Track Eligibility Status

Switch to the "2. Dashboard" tab. Here you can see if the Payton team has Approved, Rejected, or requested more info for the patient.

3. Set Up Discharge Details

Once approved and the patient is discharged, click "Setup Discharge". Enter the final billed amount, choose AutoPay or Full Pay, and attach the bank docs.

4. Request Document Pickup

Click "Req Pickup" when the physical file is ready. The system will generate a secure 4-digit PIN. You must give this PIN to the logistics driver when they arrive.
